# 👾Surveilled

A short 2D point and click game created for the <b><a href="https://pixelforgejam.org/"> 2026 Pixel Forge Game Jam</a></b>.
<p>
  Partake in corporate espionage as you sabotage your rival's applications and programs through the usage of malware.
  Keep them under your watchful gaze, but ensure you stay out of theirs.
</p>
